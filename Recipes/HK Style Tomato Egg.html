港式番茄蛋

Serves 2 People Cook Time: 15 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoon of Oil (corn/canola/vegetable)
  • 5 Roma Tomatoes or 3 large tomatoes
  • 2 Cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • 1tsp pepper
  • 4oz Water
  • 3 Oz Slurry (2Tablespoons of cornstarch + 2 Oz water mixed together)
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3 Tbs Fine Sliced Green Onions

Instructions

  1. Slice tomatoes into fourths and those fourths in half
  2. Combine all dry ingredients
  3. Whisk 3 eggs together eaten well beaten
  4. Heat on high pan until the oil smokes lightly
  5. Slide in tomatoes and cook until tender
  6. Turn down to medium high heat
  7. Add garlic
  8. Add water
  9. Add dry ingredients
  10. Saute and stir for 3 minutes or until tomatoes are extremely soft
  11. Crush tomatoes and add slurry and stir until thickened ( 1 minute)
  12. Slowly drizzle eggs in without stirring for 30 sec and then stir
  13. Optional ( Garnish with fine sliced green onion)
